About Us

Michal Chelbonani, owner of the studio and a Pilates instructor using the Stott Pilates method. Born in 1983, Michal is a professional dancer and was a choreographer in the past. Graduated with 5 years of cum laude from a choreographic college in Norway. Graduate of Basi Pilates School, Norway. Upon graduation, she joined Basi’s teaching staff. Michal graduated from a course where she worked with pregnant and postpartum women, using the "support pyramid" method for stabilization and rehabilitation of the pelvic floor. Michal also graduated the Wingate Institute, College of Physical Education and Sports, as a design and toning instructor. In order to achieve the most effective results in the shortest possible time, I usually work in small groups while personally accompanying, ensuring accuracy and adjusting the exercises for the trainees. I believe that with dedication and a lot of love we can improve posture, range of motion and flexibility. Building and maintaining the body over time while improving our trainees’ lifestyle and outlook on life is part of our mission at Michal’s studio.
